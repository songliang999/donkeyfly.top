Donkeyfly Monster Survivors

Survive endless waves of monsters in this thrilling action-packed adventure!

Game Overview

Monster Survivors is an intense survival game where your goal is to stay alive as long as possible while facing increasingly difficult waves of monsters. Level up your character, unlock new abilities, and test your skills in this challenging adventure.

How to Play

Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character. Automatically attack nearby enemies and collect experience orbs to level up. Choose powerful upgrades and create the ultimate build to survive longer.

Key Features

  • Endless waves of challenging monsters
  • Multiple character upgrades and abilities
  • Progressive difficulty system
  • Engaging survival gameplay mechanics
